Challenges of Education


The International Interdisciplinary 2nd Congress of the Center for Interdisciplinary Research at the Faculty of Philosophy in Osijek, titled "Challenges of Education" (INTER_CON 2022), will take place on November 3rd, 4th, and 5th, 2022. The congress is organized by the Center for Interdisciplinary Research at the Faculty of Philosophy in Osijek and the Faculty of Philosophy of Josip Juraj Strossmayer University in Osijek.

The congress will be held in a hybrid format, both online via the Zoom platform and in person from the Small Council Hall of the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences (Room 39), from where the lectures will also be broadcasted via Zoom
