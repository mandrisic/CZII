Interdisciplinarity in Theory and Practice


The International Interdisciplinary 1st Congress of the Center for Interdisciplinary Research of the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences in Osijek, titled 'Interdisciplinarity in Theory and Practice,' will take place on November 4th and 5th, 2021. The organizers of the congress are the Center for Interdisciplinary Research of the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences in Osijek and the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences of Josip Juraj Strossmayer University in Osijek, while the sponsors of the event are the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences of Josip Juraj Strossmayer University, the City of Osijek, and the Osijek-Baranja County.

The congress will be held in a hybrid format, both online via the Zoom platform and in person from the Small Council Hall of the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences (Room 39), from where the lectures will also be broadcasted via Zoom
